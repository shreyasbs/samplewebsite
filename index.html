About Us

Bharath Group, headquartered in Bangalore, India, is one of the country's fastest expanding conglomerates. The principally engaged sectors are real estate, infrastructure, logistics, energy, pharmaceuticals, and mining. Mr. Chetan Prakash Tayal established the Bharath Group in 1984. Under the direction of Mr. Chetan Prakash Tayal, the group has established a standard for others to emulate since its inception. We have placed "Clientele" as the focal point of our group, and all of our operations are motivated by the customer preferences, objectives, and ambitions.

Bharath Group, with over three decades of experience, has indeed been classified as highly seasoned in terms of quality and execution.
